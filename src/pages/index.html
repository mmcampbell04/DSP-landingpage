Meet The Squirel 👋

The artist formerly known as 'The Squirel' AKA 'DSP' AKA 'DS Productions' is the brainchild of Keith Barnett.

Dead Squirel Productions was born on a cold Canadian winter's night, from reams of holiday photos and video content, ready access to Youtube and the drive of 'why not?'. DSP is now a recognised production house in Keith's adopted hometown, Montreal.

Courtesy of the Squirel's East London roots, DSP also has offices in central London as well as Spain.

Want to collaborate?Get in touch!

The squirel creates videos. Lots of videos.

The Squirel's specialities include stunning holiday videos, showcasing the beauty as well as the behind the scenes of 'dream destinations' all across the globe.

He's equally at home getting down and dirty with a Go-Pro for intense action (and accident...) shots.
